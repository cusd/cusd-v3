We would love any of your in kind donations. Please email cu.sustainabledesign@gmail.com and we will work with you.

Monetary Donations

Through Cornell's Donation website, you can directly contribute to Sustainable Education Ghana. Click the link below and

  1. Under "Special Instructions," write SEG
  2. Select your preferred type of donation (one-time, recurring, scheduled payments)
  3. If you would like to make your donation in honor of someone, please fill out the next page. If not, hit continue.
  4. Complete your personal and billing information and confirm your donation
From all of us at SEG and VAM, thank you for your contribution towards making this school a reality!
